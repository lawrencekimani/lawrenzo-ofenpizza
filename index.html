The Ingredients



"High quality ingredients selection.."

Flour

We are proud to be using one of the best and highest quality pizza flours on the market, American flour is all about gluten: ready for action and headed for the highest rise. Exceptionally fine-textured, our version of Italian "00" flour is a little more laid back. Lower in protein and mellower, it yields the friendliest, gentlest dough to work with: supple, smooth, and easy to shape. The resulting baked goods are light, airy, and have a crisp crust. It's ideal for pizza, flatbreads, focaccia, and crackers.

Pesto Tomato Sauce

To me, a good pesto pizza should have bold pesto flavor. I don’t want it to taste faintly of basil, garlic, Parmesan and olive oil—I want those flavors to bowl me over. The key is to spread a generous layer of pesto over the pizzas before baking, and then top them with more pesto when they come out of the oven. Not only do these pizzas taste very “pesto-y,” they also look gorgeous and feel a little more special than your typical pizza pie..

Mozzarella.

Mozzarella is considered the best cheese for pizza for a few reasons: its delicate, milky flavor, its smooth, elastic texture, and its fabulous meltability. The texture comes primarily from the fact that it is a pasta filata-type cheese (“spun paste” in Italian)..

ABOUT US...





What could be more Kenyan than a pizza place started by three students from Nairobi? They knew that in Kenya you either go big or you go home, so they invented the Triple-Decker® and the Crammed-Crust®. They knew that if they didn’t deliver it hot and fresh, they’d be told off in 11 different languages. That’s how the Hot or Not sticker came about. They knew what a nightmare Nairobi traffic is, so they were the first to bring you free delivery and online ordering. And when they grew to 500 stores and 27.5 million pizzas a year, they knew it was time to give back. They started Doughnation, where leftover pizza dough is made into flatbreads to feed millions more. Most of all, they knew their pizza had to be amazing. Because it was made for people just like them. Deutsch Ofenpizza. Made for KENYA. So, try something unique. Try something tasty. Try something fresh. Try something amazing. Try Lawrenzo Ofenpizza.
